HEALING KNIT

Versatile Cabinet

A combination of our concept 'Healing' and
'Knit' for the knitting which is used in our furniture.

We tried to put our concept 'Healing' into the old furniture which was used as kid's chair.
To design 'Healing', we enhanced the naturalness and coziness.

For the materials we used rattan, macrame, and wood stain.

On the 1st floor we have sturdy rattan shelf which can hold heavy stuff.
On the 2nd floor we have macrame shelf which
gives more softiness and coziness than the rattan shelf.
On the back of the chair we have macrame pocket which
can hold small belongings such as earphones and car keys.

SIZE

W250 X D290 X H510

MATERIALS

rattan, macrame, wood stain

material

Macrame

Unlike the crochet which needs needle to make, macrame only needs our own bare hands.

Rattan

It is a light weighted and rough textured which is a thin pliable stems of a climbing palms from Calamus. It is used making things such as a chair, backet, and a thick rope.

Wood Stain

It protects the wood and reveals the wood grain seeping into the wood.
