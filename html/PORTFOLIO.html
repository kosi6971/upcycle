HEALING

To heal is necessary in our lives.

In competitive modern society,
it is easy to have a built-up fatigue.

However not many people know the way to heal.
It is because everyone has their own way to heal.

To let people find their way to heal,
we are willing to design the beginning of 'Healing'.

IDEA SKETCH

RENDERING

SIZE

W250 X D290 X H510

MATERIALS

Rattan, Macrame, wood stain

Before we get into the process,
We had to choose and prepare the materials.
We chose the macrame for being cozy
and rattan for being sturdy.
We have also chose wood stain to make the furniture darker.

We have separated the furniture
to paint the wood stain.
We tried to paint without separating,
but it was inevitable.

We used the walnut colored wood stain for the paint.
We repeatedly painted and dried 3 times.
Then we applied the varnish to make it shine.

Using the glue, we assembled
the separated parts of the furniture.

With the rattan, we made a shelf at the bottom.
To use the rattan, we had use water
to wet the rattan to bend it easily.

When we finished making our furniture,
it looked quite different then we expected.
The pocket was too small that we made 2 times again.
Since our plans for the process were poor,
we had to think the small details.
